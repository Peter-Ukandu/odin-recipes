Plantain Chips

Description

Ingredients

  • 5 unripe plantains, peeled
  • salt to taste
  • vegetable oil, for deep frying

Steps

  1. Cut the plantains lengthwise into thin discs.
  2. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or a medium-sized frying pan to 190 degrees Celsius.

  3. Deep fry the plantain slices, about a dozen at a time until golden brown on both sides, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain in a large bowl lined with paper towels, and salt to taste while still warm. You can add other spices if you'd like.
